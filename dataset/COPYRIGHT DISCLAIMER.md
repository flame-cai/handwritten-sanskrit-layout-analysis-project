## Fair Use Rationale 
This dataset is provided under the fair use doctrine for the following transformative, non-commercial purposes:
1) Transformative Research Purpose: This dataset serves as a benchmark to advance Graph Neural Network (GNN) based Optical Character Recognition (OCR) methodologies for historical manuscripts—a transformative scientific purpose that differs fundamentally from the original archival and preservation function of the source materials.
2) Educational and Research Use: The dataset facilitates academic research and education in machine learning, computer vision, and digital humanities—activities.
3) No Market Substitution: We are not competitors to the Lalchand Research Library. This dataset does not diminish the market for or value of the original materials.
4) Public Interest: Advancing OCR technology for historical manuscripts serves the broader public interest by improving accessibility of cultural heritage materials and enabling new forms of scholarship.
5) Minimal Reproduction: This lazy dataset contains only metadata and reference links, requiring users to interact directly with the copyright holder's platform to access any copyrighted content.
